Our school believes in hands-on learning and the importance of building a professional portfolio right from day one. Our students engage in extracurricular activities that go beyond classroom walls, immersing themselves in a vibrant community that extends to the heart of the fashion and design industry.

With strong industry connections, our students collaborate with luxury houses, industry experts, and participate in hands-on projects as part of their coursework. From fashion photography and videography to creative direction, fashion weeks, and design competitions, our students gain invaluable industry experience. Armed with confidence and a robust portfolio developed through classroom and real-world experiences, our graduates are well-prepared to thrive in the professional environment.

The Miami School of Fashion & Design

Istituto Marangoni Miami’s vision is to always be at the center of style, to drive students’ ambitions and dreams through creativity and innovation, to support creative industries worldwide, and to provide a solid and cross-disciplinary approach to education, research and business solutions in the world of Fashion and Design.
